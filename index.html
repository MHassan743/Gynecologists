BEST GYNECOLOGY

Dr. Fadia Ishtiaq

Dr. Fadia Ishtiaq is a senior consultant gynaecologist and obstetrician with over 16 years of clinical experience.

She believes that women make the best decisions for themselves when they are supported with compassionate care and clear information.

Together with her team, she strives to create a warm and respectful environment where women feel comfortable sharing their concerns and exploring their options at their own pace.

Her approach is based on clear communication, gentle guidance, and shared decision-making, ensuring that choices are made with confidence rather than being imposed.

Her vision is empowering women to make informed health decisions, supporting healthier women, families, and communities.

You can easily book by clicking the Appointment button on the top right, or using the booking form above. We are available 24/7 for support.

We offer complete pregnancy tracking, gynecology checks, nutritional plans, and pediatric services for the well-being of the mother and baby.
